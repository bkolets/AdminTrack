Features

Everything you need to stay in control

One managed package. No code. No external databases. Just full visibility into your Salesforce org.

⏱️

Automated Polling

Configure your poll interval from 5 minutes to 24 hours. AdminTrack runs quietly in the background, capturing every setup change on schedule.

💬

Instant Slack Alerts

Changes are posted to your Slack channel the moment they're detected — via a simple Incoming Webhook. No third-party integrations required.

🔍

Searchable Audit Log

Every captured change is stored in AdminAuditLog__c inside your org. Filter by date, section, user, or keyword — right within Salesforce.

🚫

Exclusion Rules

Silence the noise. Create rules to skip changes by section, action, or user — so your team only gets alerted about what truly matters.

🎛️

Control Panel LWC

Activate, pause, or trigger a manual run with a single click from a clean Lightning Web Component — no Setup navigation required.

🔒

Secure by Design

All configuration lives in a Protected Hierarchy Custom Setting. No data ever leaves your org. Works entirely within Salesforce governor limits.
